Baked goods + coffee = our love language

Tucked between the Main Street Nursery and Jack's Hardware makes for an idyllic location to enjoy one of our freshly baked treats and a thoughtfully crafted beverage. Enjoy a blueberry scone and cortado on our back patio, or take your treats and coffee to go. Don't forget to stop by with your dog and get a pup-cup and dog biscuit, always on the house!
